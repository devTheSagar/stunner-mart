How To Buy

For single Product

Step-1
Select a product that you love.

Step: 2
Chose variants and quantity, Then press on ‘Buy Now’ button.

Step: 3
Check Your product & Order details, then press ‘Pay Now’ button,

Step: 4
Chose Your Payment method

For multiple Products

Step: 1
Select products that you love.

Step: 2
Chose variants and quantity, Then press on ‘Add to cart’ button.

Step: 3
Click on ‘cart’ Icon

Step: 4
Go to the cart, and mark your item.

Step: 5
Then press ‘Proceed To Check Out’ button,

Step: 6
Check Your product & Order details, Then press ‘Pay Now’ Button.

Step: 7
Chose Your Payment method

Payment Proceed

For Bank

Step: 1
Click the ‘Credit card or Debit card’ icon

Step: 2
Fill the all information on the blank gap.

Step: 3
Press on the ‘Pay Now’ Button.

Cash on Delivery

Step: 1
Click the ‘Cash On Delivery’ icon

Step: 2
Press the ‘Confirm Order’ Button

Step: 3
Pay Cash On Delivery after receiving your product at your home!

Payment From Mobile Banking Apps

Step: 1
Open your mobile banking app on your phone

Step: 2
press ‘payment’ option

Step: 3
Enter the Account Number

Step: 4
Enter the bill amount.

Step: 5
Fill the Reference box by your Name & Mobile number.

Step: 6
Enter the counter number: 1

Step: 7
Now Enter Your Account PIN number to confirm Done!

Step: 8
You’ll receive a confirmation message
